Let's get started

This is a little farming game!

The idea is to buy as many animals as you can, as quickly as possible

You can buy the following items:

  • Chickens for 50 coins each
  • Cows for 120 coins each
  • Pigs for 115 coins each
  • Sheep for 100 coins each

Animals can be sold, if you'd like. However, they are sold for half the price you bought them for!

Animals produce products, the type and quantity of products is for you to discover

After a minute has passed the animals will have created enough products for you to collect, be careful though, if you try to collect products too early you'll break them and have to start the count down again. Any products made will last for 10 minutes then expire (in other words: the number of products to collect will stack up but only for a maximum of 10 minutes)

You'll notice that some of the animals turn funny colours, this is because they represent a multiple of that animal. For example, a dark red cow represents 10 normal cows. You will still collect all the products for 10 cows, but only one dark red cow will appear in your farm. This is for your benefit (think of the lag with 1,000,000 cows on your screen). The colour will change with each multiple of 10.

Can you find out how many animals you need to get the golden version? Wait, you already know? Okay, the real challenge now is to see how quickly you can get one of animal type in gold

Good luck! Press the button below to get started!
